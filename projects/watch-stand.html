Stand

2020   |   William Wright Guinane

Remember to stand! It seems as though my watch could not follow its own advice. When charging, the watch lay in a heap of cord on the ground, looking very messy and making it difficult to plug and unplug. To solve this problem, I designed and built a stand.

To get an idea of how big the stand would be, I took some measurements of the watch. Using these measurements, a prototype was made out of wood. This was necessary because of the watchâ€™s irregular shape. The prototype made it easy to determine exactly how wide the base should be, which was then used to create a SketchUp model. Finally, the model was then printed on 3D printers.

The stand is a cylindrical cylinder cut off part way up. This shape allows for use from any direction. Inside, a channelhas been left hollow
