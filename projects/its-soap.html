It's Soap

2020   |   Personal Project

Why does my soap bottle have as many contours as a Mercedes and why is it so hard to see whether the label says shampoo or conditioner? This simple, color coordinated package design with large lettering makes it easy to see which soap is which while taking a shower. The prototype was made with an old shampoo bottle and red spray paint with lettering done in acrylic craft paint and proves that soap packaging can be legible.
