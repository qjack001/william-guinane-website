Uuni Pro Pizza Oven Ornament

2019   |   William Wright Guinane

Design Challenge

Every year during Christmas, my family exchange handbuilt ornaments that serve as reminders of each other’s years. Less to decorate the tree, the ornaments let us look back and reflect every year at the things we have done.

Approach

During 2019, my father purchased an Uuni Pro pizza oven. With this, he fulfilled his long time dream of becoming a pizza chef. I decided this would be his ornament.

To construct the oven I used a 3D SketchUp model built using measurements taken from my Dad’s Uuni Pro Pizza Oven to ensure the unusual surfaces and angles would all meet cleanly. Next, I cut chipboard to the correct proportions and then covered it in aluminum foil to give the effect of a metal body. Details were added with popsicle sticks, paper, a fine tip Sharpie, and acetate paper.
